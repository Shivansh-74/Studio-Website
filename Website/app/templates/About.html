About Us

Welcome to Studio Laxmi, a premier photography studio located in Ghamapur, Jabalpur. With years of experience in capturing life's most cherished moments, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional service and timeless photographs that you can treasure forever.

Conveniently situated near the Belbag Police Station, we operate every day from 12:00 PM to 9:00 PM, ensuring flexible scheduling for our clients. Whether it's a wedding, a family portrait, or a creative photoshoot, our team of experienced professionals is passionate about making every event special.

At Studio Laxmi, we believe in creating memories that last a lifetime. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the photography industry. Rated 4.8 stars by our clients, we pride ourselves on offering a wide range of tailored services to meet diverse needs, including destination wedding shoots, portrait photography, and more.

Our talented photography team combines technical expertise with a deep passion for the art of photography. They are known for their ability to make clients feel comfortable, ensuring every session is a unique and enjoyable experience.

Choose Studio Laxmi for all your photography needs, and let us help you tell your story through stunning visuals.
